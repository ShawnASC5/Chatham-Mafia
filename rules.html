Rules

Killer

The Killer must eliminate all other players.
If they are shot by a Sheriff, they are knocked out for 45 seconds.

Medic

The Medic can revive dead players by touching them.
If they are shot twice, they are knocked out for a minute.

Survivor

The Survivor must survive until the end of the game.
If they are shot, they are knocked out until a Medic revives them.

Sheriff

The Sheriff can shoot a Killer to knock them out for 45 seconds.
If they are shot twice, they are dead and another player can pick up their gun.



The game is recommended to be played with 4 or more people. If there are more than 5 people, there should be two killers.
Each game is 20 minutes long. At the end, there is a 5 minute round of sudden death if there are survivors remaining.
During sudden death, the medic loses their ability to heal and the Sheriff's shots only stun the killer for 30 seconds.
